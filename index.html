Our goals are to save 100 million lives from cardiovascular disease and to prevent epidemics.

September 10, 2018

A group of global experts met in Geneva on September 10 to brainstorm how to best act on preparedness data collected through country monitoring and evaluation systems.

September 7, 2018

80 million people could have their blood pressure controlled if they switch to fixed-dose combination pills. Read more in this Lancet editorial, co-authored by our team members Dr. Tom Frieden and Dr. Marc Jaffe.

August 27, 2018

The world’s leading infectious disease experts and country health officials gathered in Atlanta in August for the ICEID conference. Attendants were able to learn more about their country’s preparedness for the next epidemic through custom demos of PreventEpidemics.org

August 14, 2018

Dr. Marc Jaffe co-authored an editorial for the Journal of the American Medical Association, stressing the importance of fixed-dose combination therapy for the control of blood pressure on a global scale.

August 6, 2018

We met with representatives of the African Field Epidemiology Network: they are a non-profit alliance dedicated to helping Ministries of Health in Africa build strong, effective, sustainable programs.

July 20, 2018

In July, WHO Viet Nam and Resolve to Save Lives met with local clinicians to support the launch of Viet Nam’s hypertension control efforts.

July 17, 2018

Viet Nam committed to improve blood pressure control rates and reduce salt intake during a launch workshop in Ha Noi on July 17. The Ministry of Health, World Health Organization and Resolve to Save Lives will work together to select hypertension treatment protocols and establish ways to evaluate their impact. Read more...

July 2-6, 2018

The Resolve to Save Lives tech team ran our first real-world pilot of the Simple Android application, which aims to help healthcare workers to longitudinally track their hypertensive patients.

June 22, 2018

Resolve to Save Lives launched PreventEpidemics.org, an interactive website which spotlights gaps in epidemic preparedness and highlights actions that countries, donors, activists and organizations can take to fill them.

May 30, 2018

Dr. Marc Jaffe traveled to China to meet with partners at the World Bank and others, and to advance the hypertension prevention work. In this photo Dr. Jaffe is having his blood pressure measured by Professor Liu Lisheng of the Beijing Lisheng Health Foundation.

May 24, 2018

At the 2018 World Health Assembly, Dr. Tom Frieden participated in a panel discussion entitled “Reducing Sugar, Salt and Fat to Prevent NCDs.” Topics presented and discussed included the benefits of banning trans fat, Vital Strategies’ sugary drinks and sodium-reduction campaigns and case studies of industry efforts to block public health programs.

May 14, 2018

The Lancet published "REPLACE a roadmap to make the world trans fat free by 2023" written by Dr. Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus and Dr. Tom Frieden

May 12, 2018

The World Health Organization announced REPLACE, a ground-breaking initiative developed in partnership with Resolve to Save Lives, which aims to eliminate trans fats from the global food supply by 2023. Read about it in The New York Times, CNN, The Wall Street Journal, or Vox.

May 1, 2018

Dr. Chris Lee traveled to Nigeria to support the government's development of their National Action Plan for Health Security.
